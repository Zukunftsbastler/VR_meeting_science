# Paper 3: Digital Charisma and Influence in VR Leadership

## Project Focus
This paper explores the human element of high-stakes professional interaction. It investigates how leadership, trust, and persuasion are affected when physical cues are filtered through VR avatars, focusing heavily on the shift toward acoustic and digital charisma.

## Local Data Dependencies
* `Sources Charisma.csv`, and external literature on acoustic profiling, digital rhetoric, and avatar trust.

## Sprints / Work Packages
### Sprint 1: Charisma Factor Extraction
* Review literature to isolate physical charisma markers lost in VR vs. acoustic/digital markers that remain or are amplified.

### Sprint 2: The Avatar Filter Analysis
* Analyze the psychological impact of avatar use on trust-building and perceived authority in professional settings (e.g., negotiations, sales).

### Sprint 3: Acoustic Profiling Synthesis
* Develop a model for "Acoustic Charisma" in VR, highlighting the importance of voice modulation, latency, and spatial audio.

### Sprint 4: Mitigation & Strategic Guidelines
* Formulate strategies for leaders and negotiators to adapt their communication style for maximum impact in VR environments.

### Sprint 5: Manuscript Drafting
* Write the scientific article focusing on the psychological and rhetorical shifts required for effective leadership and persuasion in virtual reality.
