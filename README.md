# Master Project: VR Meeting Research Trilogy

## Project Overview
This workspace contains a comprehensive, three-part research program analyzing the viability, structural requirements, and psychological dynamics of professional meetings in Virtual Reality (VR). The overarching goal is to define how, when, and why VR should be utilized in business contexts.

## The Trilogy Structure
The research is divided into three distinct but interconnected scientific papers, located in their respective subdirectories:

1. **`/01_tech_emotion` (Technology & Emotion):** Evaluates current/future VR capabilities to transmit nonverbal communication and interpersonal emotions, utilizing a longitudinal Business Intelligence forecasting approach.
2. **`/02_taxonomy_dimensions` (Theory & Taxonomy):** Decodes the DNA of business meetings by establishing a dimensional framework (e.g., abstract vs. physical, pre-determined vs. creative) to objectively measure VR suitability.
3. **`/03_charisma_leadership` (Psychology & Leadership):** Investigates how digital and acoustic charisma survive the avatar filter, focusing on trust and influence in high-stakes VR interactions.
